## â“ Decision Making Framework

### Questions to ask before deciding:

1. **5 Years From Now:**
   - Where do I want to be?
   - Which job gets me there faster?
   - What skills will I have?

2. **Risk Assessment:**
   - What's the worst-case scenario?
   - Can I afford the risk?
   - What's my backup plan?

3. **Personal Priorities:**
   - What matters most right now?
   - How does this fit my lifestyle?
   - What am I willing to sacrifice?

4. **Market Conditions:**
   - How's the industry doing?
   - Are there more opportunities?
   - What if I wait 6 months?
